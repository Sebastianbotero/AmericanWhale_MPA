# AmericanWhale_MPA
This repository contains the code employed to analyze the conservation opportunities and overlap with human activities of a proposed Marine Protected Area to safeguard the critically endangered American Whale (Balaenoptera ricei) in the Gulf of Mexico. 

Three types of analyses are performed by the code in this repository: i)Assess the overlap between the proposed MPA and several economic activities in the Gulf, as well as current protected areas; ii) Characterize vessel transit through the proposed area and evaluate the raltive importance of this transit in the Gulf; and iii) Explore the impact of protection measures for the American whale within the proposed MPA on shipping activity in the Gulf.

All analyses were based on the following publicly available datasets. 

- Rice’s Whale Core Distribution Area Map & GIS Data: https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/resource/map/rices-whale-core-distribution-area-map-gis-data 

- Proposed Rice’s Whale Critical Habitat: https://www.arcgis.com/home/item.html?id=f85d90527131406489ce721b2b71960b 

- Offshore Oil and Gas Platforms: https://hub.marinecadastre.gov/datasets/noaa::offshore-oil-and-gas-platforms/about 

- Shipping Fairways, Lanes, and Zones for US waters: https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/shipping-fairways-lanes-and-zones-for-us-waters 

- Vessel Traffic AIS tracks for 2025: https://hub.marinecadastre.gov/pages/vesseltraffic 

- Cetacean and Sea Turtle Spatial Density Model Outputs from Visual Observations Using Line Transect Survey Methods Aboard NOAA Vessel and Aircraft Platforms in the Gulf of Mexico from 2003-06-12 to 2019-07-31: https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/inport/item/67830 

- Outer Continental Shelf Active Renewable Energy Leases: https://hub.marinecadastre.gov/datasets/e5a371353b4744418e57da448dac9471_0/explore?location=28.802565%2C-92.918614%2C8  

- Offshore Wind Lease Outlines: https://boem-metaport-boem.hub.arcgis.com/datasets/BOEM::offshore-wind-lease-outlines/explore?location=32.280449%2C-94.714595%2C5 

- Aquaculture Opportunities Areas Options in Gulf of America (formerly Gulf of Mexico):   https://www.arcgis.com/home/item.html?id=1e8eb15760274231896a93ce098c192d 

- The Marine Protected Areas Inventory: https://marineprotectedareas.noaa.gov/helpful_resources/inventory.html 

- De Facto Marine Protected Areas: https://marineprotectedareas.noaa.gov/dataanalysis/defacto/  

- Reef Fish Longline and Buoy Gear Restricted Fishery Management Area:  https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/resource/map/reef-fish-longline-and-buoy-gear-restricted-fishery-management-area-map-gis-data  

- Reef Fish Stressed Area Fishery Management Area: https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/resource/map/reef-fish-stressed-area-fishery-management-area-map-gis-data
